LBS College of Engineering Kasaragod (LBSCEK)

About the College

LBS College of Engineering Kasaragod is a government-controlled engineering institution located at Povval, Kasaragod, Kerala. The college is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by AICTE.

Courses Offered

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
- Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence)
- Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS)
- Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
- Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
- Mechanical Engineering (ME)
- Civil Engineering (CE)
- Information Technology (IT)

Admission

Admissions to B.Tech programs are primarily conducted through KEAM.

Other admission categories may include:

- Management Quota
- NRI Quota
- Lateral Entry

Applicants must satisfy the eligibility criteria prescribed by the Government of Kerala and APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University.
